About Our Ministry

The Ministry Foundations

God’s Awe Worship Ministries is a Bible-based, non-profit Christian ministry founded upon prayer and the Rhema Word of God. We exist to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, disciple nations, and raise a generation that worships the Father in Spirit and in Truth.

God’s Awe Worship Ministries began as a prayer fellowship in January 2020 under the leadership of Rev. Jeremy and Tabitha Mwenda. Through God's grace, it was officially established as a ministry on September 27, 2020, in Abilene, Texas, United States of America.

Since its establishment, the ministry has remained Pentecostal, indigenous, self-supporting, and self-governing. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, the ministry continues to impact people of all ages and backgrounds locally and internationally while faithfully preaching God's Word.

Hebrews 12:28

"Therefore, let us be grateful for receiving a kingdom that cannot be shaken, and thus let us offer to God acceptable worship, with reverence and awe, for our God is a consuming fire."

The name God’s Awe Worship Ministries reflects our commitment to offering God acceptable worship with reverence and awe as revealed through His Word.

From this humble beginning, God continues to expand the ministry for His glory through evangelism, discipleship, prayer, worship, missions, and compassionate outreach to communities around the world.

Foundation

Statement of Faith

01

The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is the inspired, authoritative, inerrant, and infallible Word of God. (2 Pet 1:21, 2 Tim 3:16)

02

The Holy Trinity

One God eternally existent in three separate persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. These three are one. (2 Cor 13:14)

03

Deity of Jesus Christ

His virgin birth, atoning sacrifice, resurrection, ascension, and literal pre-millennial return to earth to reign for 1,000 years with His saints. (Jn 10:30, Phil 2:6, 1 Thess 4:16-17, Rev 20:4)

04

The Church Body

The universal and local bodies acting as vehicles for corporate worship and structural fulfillment of the Great Commission. (Eph 1:22-23, Heb 10:25)

05

Salvation & Holy Spirit

Personal salvation through grace upon heart confession, coupled with the initial evidence of speaking in tongues and the fruits of the Spirit. (Acts 2:1-4, Rom 10:9-10, Eph 2:8)

06

Water Baptism

Executed strictly via complete water immersion after personal salvation to declare dying to sin and rising with Jesus in newness of life. (Matt 28:19, Rom 6:4)

07

Divine Healing & Medicine

Obtained through the atonement and active faith in our healer, Jesus Christ. We also firmly believe in professional medical care. (Jas 5:14-15, 1 Pet 2:24)

08

Satan & Eternal Judgment

Satan is a restrained entity without divine attributes. Demons will be cast out, and along with unwritten names, consigned to everlasting punishment. (Rev 19:20, Rev 20:10-15)
